How do you know?: it has the fastest time for the easiest case (sorted) and takes a long time for the hardest case (random)

sort2 uses: merge sort

How do you know?: it has similar times for the different cases (random, reversed and sorted), with relatively fast times

How do you know?: it has similar times for the different cases (random, reversed and sorted), with relatively slow times
